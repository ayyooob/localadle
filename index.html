About Localadle

Localadle is a marketplace linking food lovers with skilled home chefs. Our aim: bring fresh, affordable, and hygienic home-cooked meals to local communities. We support chefs with onboarding, hygiene checks and a simple way to reach nearby customers.


We focus on trust and food hygiene . During our London pilot we will use chef pickup, customer pickup and trusted courier partners.
